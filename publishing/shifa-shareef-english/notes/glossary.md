# Glossary

Add terms here as the manuscript develops.

## Initial Terms

- `Huzoor`: respectful title used for the Noble Prophet ﷺ.
- `Durood`: sending prayers and blessings upon the Noble Prophet ﷺ.
- `Salam`: sending peace.
- `Ummah`: the community of the Prophet ﷺ.
- `Taqwa`: God-consciousness and protective reverence before Allah <sup>عز وجل</sup>.
- `Zuhd`: detachment from worldly excess for the sake of Allah <sup>عز وجل</sup>.
